04 · Why the change

Same brand. Better data.

The original three-green palette looks great in marketing screens. In a chart with two series, it fails 8% of viewers. The fix is small: one of the greens becomes blue when it's encoding data.

⚠ Original — green + green + orange
For someone with deuteranopia, sea green and forest collapse together.
Top: normal vision. Bottom: simulated deuteranopia. The two greens become near-identical olives.
✓ Refined — blue + green + orange (data)
Blue replaces one green when it's used as a data series colour.
Top: normal vision. Bottom: simulated deuteranopia. All three series remain distinct.
